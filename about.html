Welcome to City Library

A sanctuary for knowledge, imagination, and community.

Your Gateway to Knowledge

Founded in 1923, City Library has been serving our community for nearly a century. We're dedicated to providing a welcoming space where people of all ages can explore, learn, and connect. Our diverse collection of over 250,000 books, e-books, audiobooks, and multimedia resources offers something for everyone.

As we embrace the digital age, our mission remains unchanged: to foster a love of reading, facilitate lifelong learning, and strengthen our community through accessible resources and innovative programs.

Our Mission

Empowering minds through access to knowledge and resources.

Promote Literacy

We believe in the power of reading to transform lives. Our programs encourage readers of all ages to explore new worlds through literature.

Build Community

Our library serves as a community hub where people can gather, share ideas, and participate in cultural and educational events.

Embrace Technology

We're committed to bridging the digital divide by providing access to technology and resources that empower our patrons in the digital age.

Our History

A century of knowledge sharing and community service.

1923

Foundation

City Library was established with a donation of 5,000 books from philanthropist Elizabeth Winters.

1952

Expansion

The library moved to its current location, a beautiful historic building in the heart of downtown.

1985

Modernization

The library became one of the first in the region to implement a computerized catalog system.

2010

Digital Revolution

We launched our digital collection, offering e-books, audiobooks, and online resources to our patrons.

2023

Centennial Celebration

Celebrating 100 years of serving our community with knowledge, resources, and programming.
